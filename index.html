about us
The company is located in the Central Java city of Salatiga, a small city with cool weather and beautiful scenery as well as the developing city of Semarang. Formulatrix has both a manufacturing and engineering division developing advanced automation and robotics. Over the years we have expanded our office space to multiple sites with over 100,000 square feet for engineering and manufacturing space. The company continues to experience aggressive growth with healthy profits because of our industry recognized patented technology and superior software delivery services. We maintain a small-company culture with a worldwide outlook, with offices in the United States, Europe, and Asia. We are seeking to hire people who are passionate about their work and enjoy doing it. We are rapidly expanding our employee base. We currently have more than 300 full-time employees on staff and we are seeking even more talented individuals.

Core Values

At Formulatrix, as we tirelessly investigate technologies to support efficient research we consider these core values:

Intelligence

All problems are solved most efficiently when approached in a thoughtful and logical way. In product development challenges and troubleshooting problems, we effectively eliminate potential causes of issues to quickly diagnose failures and find solutions.

Integrity

Teams and employees can trust one another fostering a productive and friendly working environment. We are always straightforward about solutions, and never mislead customers regarding the fit for their application.

Divergent Thinking

We exhaustively consider solutions and bring technologies from outside of the industry to create novel out-of-the-box solutions. We value the discovery of novel approaches to all aspects of the company and foster a dynamic and creative environment for employees.

Sustainability

We are cost conscious and aware of market price dynamics to allow for a sustainable company for years to come.

Hard Work

Everyone invests themselves in the company through 45 hours of productive work per week.
